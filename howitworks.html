We're Meal Prep Check, the comparison service dedicated to finding you the best dishes delivered to your door.

Our comparison tool is easy to use, it'll only take 60 seconds to find the meal prep & heat and eat providers that suit you best!

Simply enter your dietary requirements, the frequency of meals, the price range you've budgeted for and your postcode. We'll ask for your email only so you can revisit your options again from your inbox.

Once you've clicked submit the options listed are those that match your requirements! You can sort these by price or by rating.

If you find one you like the look of and want more info, click “Take me to the website” to see more and place your order.

Don't forget to come back every now and again to see any new options and discounts!

We compare the offerings of over 50 meal providers. We know many of these you'll never have heard of before. We also know it's long to check Google, Facebook and Trustpilot reviews when you just want tasty scran.

That's why we've come up with RATING. RATING is an independent rating/score based on fact & opinion. RATING looks at objective facts by considering things like the number of meal options a provider offers - because lets be honest, no one wants the same meals every other week. RATING also looks at subjective opinions by aggregating the reviews of current and former customers on Google, Facebook & Trustpilot and providing an average.

RATING is reviewed quarterly as we know providers often update their offerings and thus reviews can change month to month.
