How Meal Prep Check Works

The free comparison service dedicated to finding you more marvelous meals

Step One

Let us know what you're looking for

Enter your dietary requirements, frequency of meals, the price range you've budgeted for and your postcode.

We'll ask for your email so you can revisit your options again from your inbox.

Step Two

Click Submit

The providers listed are those that match your requirements.

You can filter and sort these results by price or rating. Keep scrolling to discover all of your options.

Step Three

Visit the providers website

When you've found the one for you or if you're wanting more info, click "Take me to the website" to see more and place your order.

Don't forget to come back to discover new entrants, meal options and discounts.
