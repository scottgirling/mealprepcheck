When did all this start?

The food delivery market is in its relative infancy. The market is very competitive with new brands are constantly entering the market.

It's easy to feel lost in the search for the best deal with the never ending marketing campaigns offering huge discounts to new customers. How can anyone be sure they've found the best subscription for them?

Finding the food for you when you're working towards a specific diet plan or have certain dietary requirements only make this harder. Maybe you're bulking up and looking for protein rich meals. Or maybe you're looking for low calorie/carb options to shed a few pounds. Maybe you have allergies or intolerances. Or maybe you have a plant based diet.

That's where the idea for MealVista was born. A comparison tool to easily find the food delivery brand for you.

How do we find you better, healthier and more affordable food?

We have 3 products to achieve this: MealVista Compare, MealVista Discount and MealVista Track.

MealVista Compare is our comparison tool that allows you to compare the 80+ UK based food delivery brands. Use our easy-to-use comparison tool to easily find the best food for you and save yourself bucketloads of time.

MealVista Discount finds the latest savings and promotions on offer so you can eat even better for even less.

MealVista Track is a price tracking tool that notifies you when the price of a meal delivery subscription changes.
